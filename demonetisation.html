Demonetisation in India


Description


On 8 November 2016 the NDA government demonetised 500 and 1000 currency notes thus making them not a legal tender.For the youth and children it was first such exercise and the curiosity regarding demonetisation soared.This project gives a detailed description of demonetisation in India throughout it's history from a economical point of view.
