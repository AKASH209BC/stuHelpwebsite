Maths in Health


Description


Mathematics Project trying to make the students understand how can we apply mathematics in daily life to find health of an individual.BMI is one such measure.This project involves taking real height and weight of individuals and then calculating the BMI based on the formula weight in kgs/(height in m)^2.
